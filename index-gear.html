Home in the Southwest

We started immediately on our camper van adventures and discovered a couple of improvements we needed to make:

  1. We had gear we wanted to bring which required a roof-rack.
  2. Camping in the Southwest definitely needs full on air-conditioning.
  3. Full on air-conditioning required upgraded storage batteries.

We first added a roof-rack that Jim found through promasterforum.com from Commercial Van Interiors in San Diego, CA. Secondly, we started looking into adding the air conditioning which we knew was a big job. It required an upgrade from the dry cell batteries we had to lithium batteries. We ended up taking our van to Nomadic Cooling in Phoenix, and they installed new lithium batteries and a 12-volt rooftop airconditioner.
