About

Our Van Story

After researching and obsessing over camper vans for 2 years, we finally decided to go for it when the pandemic hit in 2020. Jim found a van at a used car dealer in Canada that had been built out by Wilderness Vans. It took about 3 months to get it over the border to Great Falls, Montana due to border closures. We finally went through a broker to formally import the van rather than wait until we could drive it across the border. Our first trip was a 4-day road trip starting in Great Falls, MT, then through Yellowstone National Park, the Grand Tetons, Salt Lake City, UT, Las Vegas, NV and then home to Phoenix, AZ. Any doubts or hesitations about jumping into van life disappeared on that trip. We were hooked!
